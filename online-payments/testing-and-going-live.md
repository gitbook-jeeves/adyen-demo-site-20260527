# Testing and going live

The final step in a strong payments journey is not just "integration complete." It is knowing that a team can validate behavior, observe real outcomes, and hand off responsibility clearly at launch.

## What belongs here

* Sandbox and test-card usage
* Result-code validation
* Webhook and event verification
* Pre-launch checklist
* Clear handoff from implementation to support and operations
