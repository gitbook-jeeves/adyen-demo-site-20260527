# Risk and compliance

Adyen's global footprint means compliance content cannot be treated as peripheral. The challenge is making that material actionable for both implementation teams and operators.

## A stronger page model

- explain the business or regulatory trigger
- show which products and flows it affects
- identify the owner during implementation
- identify the owner after launch
- link to the exact operational workflow that validates compliance

{% hint style="warning" %}
Compliance docs that are technically accurate but operationally disconnected still generate support load, escalation noise, and avoidable launch friction.
{% endhint %}

## Suggested connections

- link region-specific requirements back to checkout or terminal implementation pages
- link control requirements to Customer Area workflows
- link settlement or dispute implications into reporting pages
