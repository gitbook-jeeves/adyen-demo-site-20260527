# Launch playbook

This page is the connective tissue for a cross-functional launch team.

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Choose the primary motion

Decide whether the launch is led by online checkout, in-person rollout, or a combined omnichannel program.

- Online-first teams begin in [Online payments](https://app.gitbook.com/s/N8mPgC4KWeXCjrCH11KU/)
- Store-heavy teams begin in [In-person payments](https://app.gitbook.com/s/0p9OkGWWunLheMnhFQSt/)
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Align controls and reporting

Make sure finance, support, and risk know which reports, webhooks, and dashboard workflows they will own on day one. The draft operator model lives in [Management](https://app.gitbook.com/s/Hb12ab4QZzvwDOuVoUdb/).
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Run a go-live review

Use a single review page to confirm:

- credentials and environments
- webhook coverage
- payment method scope
- store or merchant rollout plan
- reconciliation owners
- escalation contacts
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

## What is intentionally different in this demo

Instead of making the visitor infer the launch sequence from many separate pages, this page turns the sequence into a clear checklist and points into the deeper spaces only when the reader is ready.
