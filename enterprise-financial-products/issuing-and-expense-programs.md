# Issuing and expense programs

Enterprise financial products need a different entry narrative than checkout docs.

## The story this section should tell

- Issue cards for employees, contractors, or business users
- Attach spend controls and program rules to those cards
- Connect card activity to reporting and reconciliation workflows
- Keep both product and finance stakeholders in the same documentation surface

In the demo, this page exists mainly to make the nav believable and to show the breadth GitBook can represent cleanly.
